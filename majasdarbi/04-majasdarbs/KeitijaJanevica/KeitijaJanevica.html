Projects and experience

Body jewelry collection "Line stays"

The work aims to provide insight into how modern women view corsets and the possibilities of making them a body adornment. The jewelry collection was inspired by corsets of different periods. The artist has rejected the body-modifying function but preserved the visual outlines characteristic of a corset.
